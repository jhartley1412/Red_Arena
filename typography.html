Typography

Headings

Headings will be all be 18.75 pixels or 1.172 ems

Headings will all be Bitter Bold

Paragraphs/Body

All paragraphs and body text will be 22.666667 pixels or 1.417 ems. Paragraphs will all be Nimbus Sans. Paragraphs should not exceed 5 columns in length on the 12 column grid at the desktop size. The height of body text is 33.333333 pixels or 2.083 ems
